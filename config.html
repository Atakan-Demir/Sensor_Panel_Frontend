Min: 100mm - Max: 3500mm

Lower Limit: The bottom of this area is technically unsupported and therefore out of use.
Detection Area: The sensor will detect at selected intervals and output accordingly.
Positive Offset: Adds a percentage of the offset value you set to the maximum range and extends the maximum distance in the positive direction.
Negative Offset: Subtracts the percentage of the offset value you set to the maximum range and decreases the maximum distance in the negative direction.
